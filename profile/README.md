# KNVB

This is the official github page of the Koninklijke Nederlandse Voetbalbond (Dutch National Football Association). In the future we will provide technical resources to connect our fans with their teams and data. For now, please follow us at one of the following sites:

* [Official website](https://www.knvb.nl)
* [Oranje fans channel](https://www.onsoranje.nl)
* [Voetbal.nl for amateur activities](https://www.voetbal.nl/inloggen)
* [KNVB Shop](https://www.knvbshop.nl/)
* [Ticket Shop](https://ticketing.knvb.nl)
